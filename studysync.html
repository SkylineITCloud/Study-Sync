Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We collect your university email, name, and study preferences to match you with compatible study groups. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your availability and study preferences are used solely for matching you with study groups and personalising your dashboard experience.

3. Chat & Messages

Messages in group chats are visible only to group members. We do not read or share your private messages.

4. Progress Data

Study hours and progress are stored locally and on secure servers. You can export or delete your data at any time from your profile settings.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication only. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact privacy@studysync.edu
